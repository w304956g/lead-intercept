The process

Five steps.
Fully automatic.

From the moment a lead submits to the moment your salesperson gets the warm handoff — here's exactly what happens.

01

Lead arrives from any source

Cars.com, AutoTrader, your dealer website, CarGurus — any source you choose. You control which sources get intercepted and which go straight to your CRM as-is.

Automatic
02

AI texts back in under 30 seconds

A personalized, conversational text goes out immediately — using the customer's name, the specific vehicle they asked about, and one qualifying question. Not a template. A real message.

AI powered
03

AI continues the conversation (if they reply)

The AI qualifies the lead — timeline, trade-in, financing preference — keeping the conversation warm until the customer is ready to take the next step.

AI powered
04

Two-way contact confirmed

The moment the customer replies, the system detects two-way contact and triggers the handoff. This is where AI stops and your team takes over.

Your salesperson receives an alert with the customer's name, vehicle interest, and the complete conversation transcript — already pushed into your CRM. They walk in knowing everything.
